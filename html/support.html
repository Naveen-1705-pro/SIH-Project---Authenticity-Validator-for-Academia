Frequently Asked Questions

How does the blockchain verification work?

Our system creates a unique digital fingerprint of each document and stores it on a tamper-proof blockchain network. This ensures that any modifications to the original document can be instantly detected during verification.

What file formats are supported for upload?

We currently support PDF, PNG, and JPG file formats. Each file must be under 10MB in size and should be a clear, readable image of the original document.

How long does the verification process take?

Most verifications are completed within 2-5 minutes. Complex documents or those requiring additional authentication may take up to 15 minutes.

Is my document data secure and private?

Yes, we use advanced encryption and only store document fingerprints, not the actual documents. All data is processed securely and in compliance with government privacy regulations.

What happens if a document fails verification?

If a document fails verification, you'll receive a detailed report explaining the issues found. You can contact your institution or our support team for assistance in resolving any problems.
